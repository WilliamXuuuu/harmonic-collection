Entry 3 — My Fitness Journey

I started my fitness journey in June 2023, and it has been one of the most important decisions in my life. Fitness is more than just building a stronger body—it gives me discipline, confidence, and positivity. Training every day is not only joyful, but also helps me connect with friends, support my mental health, and push through difficult times. The gym has taught me what self-discipline truly means, and how important it is to stay consistent. It has reshaped my body and, more importantly, my mind, and continues to teach me valuable lessons every day.
