Entry 5 — Photo Dump

A quiet collection of unplanned moments — studio lights, reflections, screens, sky, movement and stillness. These fragments don’t demand explanations; together they form a rhythm I live in.

City street

midday traffic, straight ahead

Blue sky

buildings under a clear day

Airport to JFK

geometry, gate 41, late night

Coffee shop

table, cup, small pause

Studio wall

works pinned for review

Trees & skyline

light after the rain

Coffee sign

this week needs coffee

By the river

downtown skyline behind

Subway ride

orange seats, late train

Maybe these are just photos. Or maybe they’re how I remember myself.
