Entry 7 — The Rhythm of My Days

My days in New York follow a soft rhythm that ties together school, art, and movement. This entry records one ordinary day — from morning to night — in small moments.

8:00 AM daily

Waking up & cardio

I usually wake up around eight or nine, sometimes even at eleven if I don’t have a morning class. I scroll for a bit, get changed, and head out for cardio. It wakes me up and gives me a sense of discipline for the rest of the day.

11:00 AM daily

Lunch & quick catch-up

Around eleven, I grab lunch at the school cafeteria and chat with friends for a bit — a small break before the afternoon really starts.

2:00 PM daily

Café focus block

I like staying at a café near campus to do assignments and listen to music. I usually order a hot latte — it’s cozy and keeps me focused.

6:30 PM daily

Print session & class

In the evening, I print my assignments — my typography class has a lot of print work this semester. After printing, I grab dinner and go straight to class.

10:00 PM daily

Wind-down

Around ten, I’m back in my dorm. I shower, lie down, check my phone or play some games. If there’s homework I finish it; if not, I just listen to music and fall asleep.
